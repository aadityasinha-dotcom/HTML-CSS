NIET

Institute of engineering and Technology Greater Noida is one of the exclusive Engineering and Management institutes of India's National Capital Region (NCR). Noida Institute of Engineering and Technology Greater Noida is a private, self-financed and co-educational institution established in 2001. NIET Greater Noida is affiliated with Dr. A.P.J Abdul kalam Technical University. The campus is at knowledge park in Greater Noida, 50 km away from Delhi railway station and 60km from Indira Gandhi International Airport, New Delhi
